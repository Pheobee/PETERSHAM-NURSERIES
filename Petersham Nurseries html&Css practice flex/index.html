A place of calm, in tune with nature and living positively.

Stepping into the world of Petersham Nurseries is to experience something unique. A destination like no other which stretches boundaries in what is created, sourced and served. Exciting senses and provoking thought; a place of aspiration and inspiration.

A unique lifestyle brand.

Home to restaurants, shops, a garden nursery and more.

With an established understanding of where to find the best that nature has to offer, recognising the strength and purity of natural connections, and an unrivalled passion to deliver excellence across everything we do, makes delivering beautiful experiences and memories our second nature.
